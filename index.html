Safeguard

C#, Unity

Safeguard lies on the premise that humanity lives on the back of a turtle who is slowly dying due to pollution and it is up to your team to protect the environment. I worked as the UI developer on a Scrum team of 5 to implement the graphical user interface, such as the character display, hover text, and action button design.

Try it out!

Hero's Arena is a PvP turn-based tactical combat game where players must utilize strategy and positioning to take out the opposing captain. I worked on a team of 4 and implemented the combat system, character selection and the random map generation, allowing players to enter a new area each match.
